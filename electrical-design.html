What We Deliver

Our Electrical Design Services

We provide complete electrical design packages for industrial automation projects — from initial concept schematics through to as-built documentation.

01

Electrical Schematics

Detailed circuit diagrams covering power distribution, control wiring, safety circuits and field device connections — created to IEC and BS EN standards.

02

Control Panel Design

Full panel layout drawings including component arrangement, busbar sizing, cable duct routing and thermal calculations. Designed for build, test and maintenance.

03

Cable Schedules

Comprehensive cable schedules defining cable types, routing, termination points and identification — fully cross-referenced to the schematic drawings.

04

Field Installation Drawings

Loop diagrams, hookup drawings and field wiring documentation to support installation teams and reduce site wiring errors.

05

P&ID Development

Piping and instrumentation diagrams for process applications — defining equipment, instrumentation, control philosophy and safety systems.

06

As-Built Documentation

Following commissioning, we update all drawings to reflect the as-installed condition, providing an accurate permanent record for maintenance and future modifications.
